**DriveIQ** is a long‑term personal project that I began conceptualizing in 2014, long before the tools existed for a single person to build a system of this scale. With the emergence of modern AI‑assisted development in the summer of 2024, I finally had the ability to begin implementing the vision myself. I began building the app in 2025, creating navigation structures, feature modules, and detailed product documentation.

DriveIQ is designed as a next‑generation automotive research and engagement platform that blends survey participation, personalized insights, and a unique user experience. The concept includes a multi‑layered feature ecosystem, cross‑module integrations, and a structured navigation model.

I have created extensive internal documentation covering user flows, behavioral design, analytics, and technical architecture. These materials are not published publicly but demonstrate my ability to design complex, multi‑feature systems from the ground up.

## Design Philosophy
DriveIQ is built around the idea that automotive research can be engaging, ethical, and user‑centered. The concept emphasizes privacy, transparency, and Apple‑native interaction patterns that make participation feel intuitive and rewarding.

### Status
The project is currently paused while I focus on my career transition, but it remains one of my most fully developed long‑term concepts.
